Our Production Process

1) Concept & Script

  • Creative brief, scriptwriting and storyboarding focused on message and CTA.
  • Choose tone, pacing and format for the target platform and audience.

2) Shoot or Source Footage

  • On-location or studio shoots (optional) and remote filming guides for distributed teams.
  • Stock sourcing and licensing when needed to reduce time and costs.

3) Edit & Motion Design

  • Rough cut -> feedback -> fine cut with graded color, sound design and motion graphics.
  • Deliver versions for feed, story, and landscape formats with A/B creative variants when needed.

4) Optimize & Deliver

  • Encoding for platforms (bitrates, aspect ratios), thumbnail & description suggestions, and export presets for ads.
  • Optional campaign setup and variant testing support.
